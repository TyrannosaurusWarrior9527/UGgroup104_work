Adelaide Culture

Adelaide Festival

One of Australia's largest multi-arts festivals, featuring theatre, music, dance, literature, and visual arts, held annually in March.

WOMADelaide

An iconic open-air festival celebrating music, arts, and dance from around the world, held in Botanic Park every March.

Aboriginal Art & Heritage

Adelaide showcases the rich culture of Australia's First Nations through galleries, museums, and cultural centers featuring Aboriginal stories and art.

Adelaide Fringe

The largest open-access arts festival in the Southern Hemisphere, known for comedy, cabaret, circus, and more across hundreds of venues.
